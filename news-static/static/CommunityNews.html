4 years ago
Duplicate tiddlers from tiddlywiki.com now removed

The aggregator process now removes tiddlers which are also found on tiddlywiki.com. This way authors can use the tiddlywiki.com content on their wiki, and it won't be indexed as "their" content. Additionally that makes the CommunitySearch wiki a bit faster.

See DuplicateTiddlerDetection.
